Why It Works
You're not lazy.
You're unstructured.

Most creatives don't have a motivation problem. They have a system problem. No deadlines, no witnesses, no consequences — so nothing ever ships.

TAC Co. fixes that. Every challenge gives you a clear window, a community watching, and proof requirements that make ghosting yourself impossible.

  • 01
    A community that keeps you honest When others see your work, skipping feels different. Peer accountability is the accountability that actually sticks.
  • 02
    Structure that replaces willpower 90-day rounds with defined submission windows. You always know what's due and when — no ambiguity to hide in.
  • 03
    Proof requirements that make it real No vague check-ins. Submit the screenshot, the link, the campaign — or it didn't happen.
  • 04
    Stakes that make finishing worth it Prize pools funded by entry fees. Show up every week and you compete. Miss too many and you don't.
