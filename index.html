Start Where You Are

You bring your existing ad setup — Meta, Google, or both. AAC isn't a course on how to run ads. It's the structure and accountability to run them consistently and actually review what's happening week over week.

No minimum ad spend required. Members at $10/day and $1,000/day both belong here. The discipline is the same either way.

One Active Campaign, Every Window

Each submission window, you upload a screenshot of your Ads Manager showing at least one live campaign. No active ad = no credit for that window. No exceptions, no grace period, no "I meant to."

📸 Screenshot proof required each window

Your Numbers on the Form

Along with your screenshot, you fill out the submission form each window: spend, impressions, CTR, results. It takes two minutes and builds the habit of actually reading your dashboard instead of ignoring it.

📊 Spend · Impressions · CTR · Results

One Change Tied to Data

New ad creative. Updated copy. A budget shift. A new audience segment. Whatever the numbers are telling you — you act on it and log what you changed and why, directly on the submission form. This is how ad spend compounds.

✏️ Documented on the submission form

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need before you join. More questions? Reach out directly.

Do I need to already be running ads?
+
Not necessarily. AAC is about accountability and consistency, with a side of education. You can set one up on your own when we start, but we can't tell you specifically what to do. But we can encourage you to be consistent and to not give up if you don't see results immediately.
Which platforms are supported?
+
Meta and Google Ads are the primary focus. TikTok, LinkedIn, and others are accepted as long as you can submit equivalent screenshot proof of an active campaign and weekly performance data.
What counts as a "documented change"?
+
Anything you changed based on what the data told you — new creative, updated copy, budget adjustment, new audience, bid strategy change. Describe what changed and why, and it counts.
Is there a minimum ad spend?
+
No minimum set by AAC. You need at least one active, spending campaign per week — but the budget is yours. Members at $10/day and members at $1,000/day are both welcome.
Will Nate coach us on ad strategy?
+
Not in a 1-on-1 coaching sense — AAC isn't primarily about education. It's about maintaining consistency. However, Round 1 will include monthly live strategy Zooms, guest speaker sessions with real practitioners, and peer ad reviews inside the community. The knowledge is there. The accountability structure surfaces it consistently.
How does the prize pool work?
+
The prize pool is funded by entry fees — the more members who join, the larger it gets. All AAC All-Stars (every weekly minimum, all 12 weeks of Round 1) are eligible. Final prize structure is confirmed before Round 1 opens.
Is this connected to SMAC and WAC?
+
Yes. AAC is the third challenge under The Accountability Challenge Co., alongside SMAC (social media) and WAC (websites). Same proof-based model, different discipline: paid ads.
